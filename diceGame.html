Welcome to Texas Holdem Of Dice

Rules of the game

-Each player will roll the dice to get a set of number
-The players will compare each others had to see who has the better hand
-The player with the higher hand wins!!

The Ranking of different hands type:

  1. Hand with six '1' for a score of '10' points.
  2. Hand with at least '3' Happy Number for a score of '7' points.
      3. NOTE: Happy Numbers are: 1,7,10, 13 and 19.
  3. Hand with '4' even numbers for a score of '5' points.
  4. Hand with '5' odd numbers for a score of '4' points.
  5. Hand with non of the above comenations for a score of '0' points.
